The Story of Endrobe

Endrobe was founded on the belief that education should be as unique as the individual pursuing it. Conceived as a flexible certification agency, it operates on a "learning objective-first" model, prioritizing the specific goals and needs of students across Pakistan.

The Journey of Duaa Mumtaz

I've always been a person with more than one interest. People used to say that the one who wants to become everything can't become anything, but I proved them wrong through my hard work. From sitting in international MUNs as a delegate to my clinical rounds with doctors, and from preparing for medical competitive exams to continuing my passion for computer coding simultaneously, I have learned that nothing is impossible if you really want to achieve something. No matter what your path is, if you have passion, you should cross oceans for it.

Seeing the environment of students especially in my country, where I have always been discouraged to focus on interests aside from medical studies, I wanted to make a platform where students can easily choose their majors, minors, extracurriculars, and languages at any age. These options should not be for university students only; this is the bare minimum every student needs. That's how this platform came into being.

Beyond my academic interests, I am deeply invested in humanitarian work through "ParhoPakistan," an initiative I founded to donate books and stationery to underprivileged students. I believe everyone should have a fair chance to learn, which is why I started teaching underprivileged students free of cost. I not only integrated my skills in my studies but also in extracurriculars. I started writing novels at a very young age; this writing skill led me to medical research and articles. I was always passionate about human wellness and co-founded a mental health awareness initiative, "Clarytin."
