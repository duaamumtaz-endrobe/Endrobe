The Story of Endrobe

Endrobe was founded on the belief that education should be as unique as the individual pursuing it. Conceived as a flexible certification agency, it operates on a "learning objective-first" model, prioritizing the specific goals and needs of students across Pakistan.

The Journey of Duaa Mumtaz

My journey is a blend of academic pursuit, creative expression, and a deep commitment to social impact. Throughout my experiences, I have learned that growth happens at the intersection of passion and service. Whether I am conducting medical research or engaging in the fast-paced world of international Model United Nations and competitive debates, I am constantly seeking new ways to challenge my perspective and contribute to the community.

Beyond my academic interests, I am deeply invested in humanitarian work through "ParhoPakistan," an initiative I started to donate books and stationery to underprivileged students, ensuring everyone has a fair chance to learn. My creative side is equally vital to who I am; I am an active calligrapher focusing on classical scripts like Thuluth and Nastaliq, a writer currently crafting my own novel and memoir, and someone who finds joy in the precision of baking. By bridging these diverse interests—from technical research to the artistry of calligraphy—I am dedicated to building a future that is as creative as it is impactful.
