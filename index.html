The Story of Endrobe

Endrobe was founded on the belief that education should be as unique as the individual pursuing it. Conceived as a flexible certification agency, it operates on a "learning objective-first" model, prioritizing the specific goals and needs of students across Pakistan.

The Journey of Duaa Mumtaz

My journey has been defined by a commitment to academic rigor and humanitarian service. From my early days in school, I have balanced intensive studies—such as my current 40-day preparation for the Aga Khan University entrance exam—with a passion for creating meaningful social change.

Beyond the classroom, I founded "ParhoPakistan" to facilitate book and stationery donations, ensuring that others have the resources they need to thrive. My path has also included presenting medical research, participating in international Model United Nations, and clinical internships at Liaquat National Hospital. Whether I am writing my novel, practicing calligraphy, or building the software foundation for Endrobe on GitHub, every project is a step toward a more objective-driven future.
